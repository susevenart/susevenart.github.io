About

Su Seven (Hui Su) is a London-based artist with a background in finance. Working between painting, installation and text, her practice examines how economic structures, global trade and algorithmic systems leak into intimate, psychological space.


She often uses blue and green tones, modular forms and fragmented diagrams to visualise flows of value, attention and desire. Rather than illustrating theory, the work tries to condense complex systems into quiet, tangible images.

Born in China · Lives and works in London.
Previous career in finance before transitioning into full-time artistic practice.
