SCULPTURE

Deficit

Sculpture series · 2023

A series of sculptures exploring erosion, meaninglessness and exhaustion in contemporary work structures, using bonsai forms, natural fragments and everyday industrial materials.

Soccer Tribe

Outdoor installation · 2023

An outdoor installation exploring self-control, attention and temptation. Football becomes a symbol of primal instinct, suspended as “prey,” inviting viewers to negotiate desire, focus and impulse in a contemporary environment.

Control

Sculpture · 2024

A sculptural exploration of boundary, pressure and bodily tension. Using metal arcs, vertical rods and industrial fragments, the work considers how invisible systems shape movement, resistance and self-regulation.

Barrier Code

Sculpture / Installation · 2024

A hybrid sculpture-installation examining obstruction, access and coded restriction. Through lattice structures, industrial bars and a small embedded panel, the work explores how contemporary systems regulate movement and behaviour, forming boundaries that are both physical and psychological.

ABOUT

Su is a London-based artist whose practice moves between sculpture, installation, painting and text. With a background in finance, she examines the invisible systems that shape contemporary life—economic flows, algorithmic structures, attention, and the perception of value.

Her work often begins with rational diagrams, market logic or technological metaphors, but gradually departs from them, entering a speculative, psychological space. Through restrained forms, modular structures and quiet colour fields, she attempts to visualise how external systems leak into the body: how they reorganise memory, intimacy and the sense of self.

Rather than representing data or illustrating theories, her practice seeks a subtler transformation—turning abstraction into something experiential, translating systemic tension into material presence.
